Meet Our Expert Instructors

Our experienced and internationally certified instructors bring years of expertise and a deep understanding of yoga philosophy to guide you on your transformative journey.

Dr. Shalini Singh

Lead Instructor & Founder

"Dr. Shalini Singh brings together the precision of medical science and the depth of holistic wellness in her yoga journey. Holding a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and a B.Sc. degree, she integrates her scientific understanding of the human body with the timeless wisdom of yoga. With over nine years of dedicated personal practice and more than two years of teaching experience, Dr. Shalini is an internationally certified yoga instructor and a registered with YACEP® (Yoga Alliance Continuing Education Provider), specializing in Face Yoga, Pre-natal, and Post-natal Yoga. Her strong medical background ensures that her classes are safe, effective, and accessible for practitioners at all levels. Loved for her warm, approachable, and mindful teaching style, she creates a nurturing space where students feel supported in their physical, mental, and emotional growth. Her classes are known for their personalized attention, thoughtful guidance, and focus on cultivating harmony of body and mind. Dr. Shalini’s genuine care and dedication have earned her the trust and affection of students worldwide, making her not just a teacher but also a compassionate guide on the path to holistic well-being"

Mrs. Kamlesh Jain

Senior Instructor

"Mrs. Kamlesh Jain is a certified yoga instructor with rich experience in training not only students but also yoga teachers, having mentored many who are now successful instructors themselves. She holds Master’s degrees in Sanskrit and Hindi, which add depth to her understanding of yogic philosophy and traditional practices. She specializes in teaching yoga to seniors, offering gentle yet effective practices to improve flexibility, mobility, and overall wellness. A dedicated practitioner, Mrs. Kamlesh is also an expert in Arham Dhyan Yoga, a unique method that integrates mantras with yoga asanas, promoting deep relaxation and spiritual harmony. Her compassionate approach and profound knowledge make her sessions deeply transformative and highly cherished by her students."

Deepak Jain

Meditation Instructor and Co-founder

"Deepak Jain brings a rare fusion of engineering precision and spiritual mastery to the world of online yoga instruction. An internationally certified yoga instructor with over a decade of immersive practice, he has journeyed deep into transformative meditation traditions including Vipassana and Kriya Yoga. What sets Deepak apart is his profound understanding of Ashtanga Yoga's philosophical foundations – the sacred 8 Limbs that form yoga's complete path. His engineering mind, shaped by degrees in Aeronautical and Mechanical Engineering, brings systematic clarity to ancient teachings, making complex spiritual concepts accessible and practical for modern practitioners. Beyond the mat, Deepak's expertise in Vedic Astrology adds a cosmic dimension to his holistic approach to wellness. His distinguished career as an Assistant Professor and Group A Scientist reflects his natural gift for teaching and his methodical approach to transformation. Experience the perfect blend of timeless wisdom and contemporary insight with a teacher who truly understands both the science and soul of yoga."
