Meet Dr. Shalini Jain

Dr. Shalini Jain brings together the precision of medical science and the depth of holistic wellness in her yoga journey. Holding a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and a B.Sc. degree, she integrates her scientific understanding of the human body with the timeless wisdom of yoga. With over nine years of dedicated personal practice and more than two years of teaching experience, Dr. Shalini is an internationally certified yoga instructor and a registered with YACEP® (Yoga Alliance Continuing Education Provider), specializing in Face Yoga, Pre-natal, and Post-natal Yoga. Her strong medical background ensures that her classes are safe, effective, and accessible for practitioners at all levels. Loved for her warm, approachable, and mindful teaching style, she creates a nurturing space where students feel supported in their physical, mental, and emotional growth. Her classes are known for their personalized attention, thoughtful guidance, and focus on cultivating harmony of body and mind. Dr. Shalini’s genuine care and dedication have earned her the trust and affection of students worldwide, making her not just a teacher but also a compassionate guide on the path to holistic well-being
